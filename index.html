About Me

I have discovered that web development combines two of my favourite hobbies: Art and Code. It is rare to find something so rewarding as a finished web site, and I have dedicated much of my time to learning the intricacies of HTML5 and CSS. Web design is the one artistic medium that I genuinely have a love for, and hopefully one day I will master.
